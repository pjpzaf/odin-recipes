Pad Thai

But still, in whatever mood that I am, Pad Thai is always a good idea. Again, I am a coder not a chef. I code so that I can afford to eat Pad Thai. Nuff said.


Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1⁄4 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1⁄8 tablespoon crushed red pepper
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 1⁄4 crushed peanuts
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Directions

  1. Heat butter in a wok or large heavy skillet. Remove, and set aside
  2. Heat oil in wok over medium-high heat
  3. Crack eggs into hot oil, and cook until firm
  4. Stir in chicken, and cook for 5 minutes
  5. Add softened noodles, vinegar, fish sauce, sugar, and pepper
  6. Adjust seasonings to taste
  7. Mix while cooking, until noodles are tender
  8. Add bean sprouts, and mix for 3 minutes
