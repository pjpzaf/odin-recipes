Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 (4 ounce) package pancetta bacon, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 21⁄2 cups whipping cream
  • 1⁄4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1⁄4 chopped fresh italian parsley
  • 4 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Cook Spaghetti
    1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
    2. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water while stirring ocassionally. Wait until tender yet firm to bite. This will take around 12 minutes (approx)
    3. Drain
  2. Saute pancetta and garlic
    1. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat
    2. Add pancetta and garlic and saute until both are brown and crispy
