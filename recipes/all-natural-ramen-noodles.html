All Natural Ramen Noodles

Image of the All Natural Ramen Noodles taken from allrecipes.com

Hopefully, it tastes better that it looks. I generally love ramen, and having been to Japan, I have tasted authentic noodles that are to die for. I don't know what to expect for this one, but I am not hard to please when you put ramen in the name of the dish ;). As per usual, I don't cook, I code!


Ingredients

  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha hot sauce
  • 9 ounces soba noodles

Directions

  1. Combine broth, water, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, and hot sauce in a pot; bring to a boil
  2. Add noodles to boiling broth mixture and cook until noodles are tender yet firmto the bite, 5 to 7 minutes
  3. Transfer noodles to serving bowls and top with desired amount of broth
